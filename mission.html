The Mission

In the ESUMS cafeteria you are required to take a fruit or vegetables. Many of the fruits and vegetables go to waste since most kids don't take them of their own accord. In "The Food Fix" the player makes sure that food does not go to waste. This shows how making the right choices leads to only good outcomes.

By decreasing the food waste you can see the trash and food waste will go down while the environmennt will get better and money will increase.
