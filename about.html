About Patrick Nardini

Patrick, a "killer" type gamer, has been coding since 2019. He is a big fan of travel, has lived abroad, and speaks multiple languages. When he's not gaming, he's working out, running, or pole vaulting. He plays 2 sports, 3 instruments, and countless video games.

    His favorite games are:
  • Rust; for its PvP aspects and immersive world generation
  • Minecraft; for its creativity, its fun with friends, and its originality
  • CS:GO; for its skill requirement, its cosmetics, and its community

    • He defines a game as: An activity with participants, an objective, played for entertainment, and that follows a set of rules.
